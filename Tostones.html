Tostones Recipe

View full Tostones recipe

Description

Tostones are twice-fried plantain slices, a popular snack and side dish in Dominican cuisine. Crispy on the outside and soft inside, they are perfect for dipping or eating on their own.

Ingredients

  • 2 green plantains
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel the plantains and cut them into 1-inch thick slices.
  2. Fry the slices in hot oil until lightly golden, then remove and flatten each slice.
  3. Return the flattened slices to the oil and fry until crispy and golden brown.
  4. Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with salt. Serve hot.
