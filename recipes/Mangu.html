Mangu Recipe

View full Mangu recipe

Description

Mangu is a traditional dish served typically for breakfast in Dominican culture. The contents of this dish are typicaly mashed green plantains, fried slices of cheese and salami and topped off with red onion slices.

Ingredients

  • 3 green plantains
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Red onions
  • Queso frito (fried cheese)
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Peel the plantains and cut them into chunks.
  2. Boil the plantains in salted water until tender.
  3. Mash the plantains with butter and a bit of the cooking water until smooth.
  4. Begin frying eggs, salami, and cheese to serve with dish
  5. Join all toppings together. Serve warm.
  6. Emjoy mangu!
