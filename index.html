Mangu Recipe

View full Mangu recipe

Description

Mangu is a classic Dominican breakfast dish made from boiled and mashed green plantains, often served with onions, cheese, or eggs.

Ingredients

  • 3 green plantains
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Red onions (optional, for garnish)
  • Queso frito (fried cheese, optional)
  • Eggs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel the plantains and cut them into chunks.
  2. Boil the plantains in salted water until tender.
  3. Mash the plantains with butter and a bit of the cooking water until smooth.
  4. Top with saut√©ed onions, cheese, or eggs as desired. Serve warm.
